Papers

Tools for Mathematical Modelling (pdf)
This paper introduces essential tools for mathematical modelling.

Infinite Hotel Paradox (pdf)
The Infinite Hotel Paradox is a theoretical scenario of accommodating an infinite number of guests, prompting discussion on the nature of infinity in relation to infinitely countable sets.

A tumbling T-handle in space: the Dzhanibekov effect (pdf)
The Dzhanibekov Effect refers to the phenomenon where the orientation of an object rotating about an intermediate axis changes unexpectedly.

Empowering Silver Shoppers: Improving the Unmanned Convenience Store Experience for Seniors in Singapore (pdf)
